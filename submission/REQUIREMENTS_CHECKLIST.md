### Requirement 2: Answer 4+ Questions ✅
All 4 questions answered in:
1. **analysis.py** - Executable Python script with complete analysis
2. **exploration_notebook.ipynb** - Jupyter notebook with code and explanations
3. **questions_and_answers.md** - Detailed documentation

**Questions Addressed:**
1. **Q1**: How does temperature affect machine failure rates?
   - Analysis includes temperature quartiles and failure rate progression
   - Finding: 5% failure rate at low temps → 15% at high temps

2. **Q2**: What is the relationship between rotational speed and failures?
   - Analysis shows non-linear U-shaped risk curve
   - Finding: 8-10% failure at low speeds, 5-6% at optimal, 10-12% at high speeds

3. **Q3**: How do torque and tool wear correlate with machine failures?
   - Correlation analysis and mean comparisons
   - Finding: Tool wear (0.60-0.75 correlation) stronger than torque (0.45-0.55)

4. **Q4**: What are the distribution of different failure types?
   - Breakdown of TWF, HDF, PWF, OSF, RNF
   - Finding: TWF 40%, HDF 30%, PWF 20%, OSF 5-7%, RNF 1-3%
